Why this role is exciting

Buildlab has recently completed a major tech refresh. Evolve, Xero and Employment Hero are now live, our AI automation roadmap is ready, and we need someone who can turn good ideas into practical systems that save time, reduce friction and help our teams do their best work.

This is a hands-on role for someone who enjoys the space between business problems, systems, people and delivery partners. You will not just advise from the sidelines — you will help make things happen.

What you’ll make happen

Embed systems: stabilise the platforms, support users and improve workflows after go-live

Bring automation to life: prioritise, brief and deliver AI-enabled automation that removes manual work

Drive partner performance: keep vendors clear on scope, timelines, accountability and outcomes

Turn problems into briefs: run workshops, capture requirements and translate business needs into build-ready specifications

Prove the value: track efficiency gains and show how technology is improving the business

You’ll enjoy this role if you are...

Commercial, curious and practical. You can talk to leaders, listen to users, challenge vendors and still get into the detail when a system, workflow or requirement needs testing.

What you’ll bring

Experience in a technology, systems, business systems or automation leadership role

Hands-on experience with construction platforms such as Evolve, Procore, Databuild or Cheops

Working knowledge of Xero and Microsoft 365

Experience managing external IT, technology or automation partners

Confidence running workshops, documenting requirements and communicating with senior stakeholders

A practical mindset — you care about outcomes, not technology for technology’s sake

Bonus points for

AI automation, RPA or workflow tools such as n8n, Make, Zapier or similar

Residential construction, land lease, property development or building industry experience

Data migration, governance or system decommissioning experience

Why join Buildlab?

Own meaningful work: help shape how a growing construction business uses technology

Be close to decision-making: work directly with senior leaders and key delivery partners

Make practical innovation real: deliver systems and automation that people actually use

Work flexibly: hybrid arrangement with 2 days in the office

If you like solving real business problems, making systems work better and seeing your work create visible impact, we would love to hear from you.

Apply with your resume and a short note outlining your experience delivering technology, systems or automation outcomes.
