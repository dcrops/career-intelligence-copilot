AI Systems DeveloperMelbourne CBD | Hybrid (4 days office / 1 day WFH)
Salary: Competitive package
Build and operate the AI systems shaping the future of fintech.

We’re partnering with an innovative Melbourne-based fintech that is transforming how businesses manage invoice payments and cash flow.

As they continue building an AI-native platform, they are looking for an AI Systems Developer to help take their AI capabilities from early development through to reliable, production-ready systems.
This is a unique opportunity for an engineer who enjoys the intersection of software engineering, artificial intelligence and platform reliability — someone who doesn’t just want to experiment with AI, but wants to build, deploy and own systems that create real business impact.

The Role: As an AI Systems Developer, you will be responsible for building and operating the production AI stack.

You’ll work across:
LLM-powered applications
AI agents and automation workflows
Data pipelines and retrieval systems
Model serving and AI infrastructure
Monitoring, observability and performance optimisation
Once AI capabilities are live, you’ll own their reliability — ensuring systems remain scalable, secure, cost-effective and performant.

This role sits within the Data, AI & Systems function and will work closely with engineering and product teams to safely embed AI into critical business processes.

What You’ll Be Doing:

Build AI capabilities into production
Design, develop and deploy AI-powered features and services
Build data pipelines, retrieval systems and AI agents
Work with LLM applications and model-serving infrastructure
Improve engineering practices to safely integrate AI into the core platform
Own reliability and performance
Monitor AI systems across uptime, latency, cost and accuracy
Build observability across metrics, logs, tracing and evaluations
Identify performance issues, model degradation and system failures
Optimise AI systems for scale and efficiency
Support production operations
Participate in incident response and troubleshooting
Develop runbooks, processes and safeguards
Implement safe deployment, rollback and change-management practices
Improve systems to prevent recurring issues
About You

You’re a strong software engineer who enjoys owning systems beyond just writing code.
You’ll ideally have:
5+ years of software engineering experience
Experience operating production systems at scale
Hands-on experience with AI/LLM applications, agents or ML systems
Strong AWS experience
Infrastructure-as-Code experience (Terraform preferred)
Experience building data pipelines and working with databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
Experience with monitoring, observability and incident response
Strong understanding of reliability, scalability and system design
Experience with the following would be advantageous:
Ruby on Rails
Domain Driven Design
Financial services or other high-reliability industries
Kubernetes / cloud-native architectures
Why Join? This is an opportunity to be part of one of Australia’s most exciting AI-driven fintech journeys.
You’ll have the chance to:
Build AI systems from the ground up
Own technology that directly impacts customers and business outcomes
Work with modern AI tooling and infrastructure
Shape how AI is implemented safely in a financial platform
Join a collaborative, high-performing engineering team
Additional benefits include:
Competitive salary and benefits package
Hybrid working model (Monday–Thursday in the Melbourne CBD office, Friday WFH)
Modern office environment
Regular team events and interstate off-sites
Clear opportunities for growth and progression
